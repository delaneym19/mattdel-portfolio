About

There are so many design resources on the web. I often found myself lost in hundreds of bookmarks and files. With this site, I try to focus on the best of the best, limiting the total links less than 100 and each category, less than 12.

If you like this approach, feel free to share it. And if you have suggestions, sites you think are important, etc., feel free to email those to me :)

This site is created with Parse, Handlebars, Flat UI Colors, Font Awesome, Montserrat, Lato, and Crimson Text.
